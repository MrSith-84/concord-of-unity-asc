The Apex Separatist Consortium

Order Through Sovereignty

The Apex Separatist Consortium is a sovereign syndicate built upon the principles of strategic autonomy, galactic reclamation, and the pursuit of forbidden knowledge. Our presence is known. Our reach is absolute.

Founding Doctrine

Born from the ashes of collapse, the Consortium is built on doctrine that prioritizes control over chaos. Every member is bound by our Prime Edict: Strength Above All, Unity Without Chains.

Operational Structure

Our organization flows through a vertical hierarchy — the Apex Council at the top, the Vassal Lords beneath, and the Field Executors across sectors. No decision escapes the eye of the Core Command Node.
