Diplomatic Procedures

Bridge Plan Framework

Every diplomatic mission follows a structured approach:

  • Objectives - Clear mission goals and success criteria
  • Red Lines - Non-negotiable positions and boundaries
  • Fallback Positions - Alternative outcomes and compromises
  • Reconciliation Deliverables - Measurable outcomes for lasting peace

Accord Ladder Process

  1. Statement of Principles - Initial agreement on fundamental issues
  2. Technical Working Groups - Detailed implementation planning
  3. Verification Protocols - Monitoring and compliance mechanisms
  4. Phased Implementation - Gradual rollout with checkpoints

Mediation Protocols

  • 72-Hour Cooling Period - Standard de-escalation window
  • Extensions - Approved by High Envoy when needed
  • Joint Signals - Coordinated communications to prevent misinterpretation
  • Documentation - All drafts preserved in the Cipher-Ledger Annex
