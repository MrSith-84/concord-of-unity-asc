Command Structure

Marshal of Peace Keepers

Service chief and Council Custodian liaison. Oversees all Peace Keeper operations and maintains direct communication with the Unity Council.

Sector Marshals

Theater command officers who coordinate with Regional Envoys and manage operations within their assigned sectors.

Specialized Units:

  • Rapid Response Groups (RRG) - Light, airliftable units for quick deployment
  • Stability & Civil Affairs (SCA) - Policing, crowd management, and civil liaison
  • Evacuation & Relief Corps (ERC) - Medevac, logistics, and engineering support
  • Signals & ISR - Unarmed surveillance for treaty verification and intelligence gathering

Rules of Engagement

Core Principles

1. Civilian-First Doctrine

Shield before sword; medical before maneuver. Protection of life and essential services above all objectives.

2. Proportionality

Force scales only to remove immediate threat; de-escalation preferred over confrontation.

3. Positive Identification

No engagement without PID and commander authorization unless in self-defense.

4. Accountability

Body-worn telemetry, incident logging, and independent review by the Ethos Commission.
