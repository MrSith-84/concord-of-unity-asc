Rules of Engagement

Core Principles

1. Civilian-First Doctrine

Shield before sword; medical before maneuver. Protection of life and essential services above all objectives.

2. Proportionality

Force scales only to remove immediate threat; de-escalation preferred over confrontation.

3. Positive Identification

No engagement without PID and commander authorization unless in self-defense.

4. Accountability

Body-worn telemetry, incident logging, and independent review by the Ethos Commission.
