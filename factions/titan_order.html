Titan Order

Disciplined warriors and tacticians, upholding order through strength and advanced technology.

Lore

The Titan Order enforces the Consortium’s will with unyielding precision. They are strategists, conquerors, and guardians of ancient weaponry, believing that discipline is the only true path to dominion.
