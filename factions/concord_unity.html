The Unity Creed

"Unity against darkness" - the desperate rallying cry that binds together fallen empires, greedy corporations, fanatical zealots, and fractured militias. Born of fear as the Consortium rose, they see themselves as guardians of civilization, the last bulwark against spreading shadow.

Yet beneath noble proclamations lies harsh reality - these are not allies united by shared ideals, but survivors bound together by mutual fear of annihilation.

The Shield Against Darkness

The Concord positions itself as the moral alternative to the Consortium's rule through strength. They claim to defend civilization itself, standing as the shield against the Consortium's blade.

However, the very fractures that drove them together may ultimately prove their undoing.
