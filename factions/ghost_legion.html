Ghost Legion

Elite operatives skilled in infiltration, sabotage, and intelligence gathering. Masters of the unseen war.

Lore

The Ghost Legion thrives in secrecy, infiltrating enemy strongholds and dismantling them from within. Their operatives are phantoms on the battlefield, striking unseen and vanishing before retribution can be delivered.
